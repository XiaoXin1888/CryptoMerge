Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 16, 2026

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the "Crypto Merge" application handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

This application is a strictly offline, local game. We do not collect your name, phone number, location data, or device identifiers to upload to any external servers.

Your game progress (including your "Best Score") is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. If you uninstall the application, this data will be deleted.

This application only requires basic permissions necessary for the game to run. It does not request access to your Camera, Microphone, Contacts, or Photos.

4. Children's Privacy

This application is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children.
